Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

BrainHive is committed to protecting your privacy while providing secure access to your study progress and session data.

Information We Collect

  • Discord account information needed for login: username, avatar, Discord ID, and email when provided.
  • Study session and Pomodoro set details used to display your dashboard and track progress.
  • Leaderboard data and usage metrics needed for bot features.

How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Authenticate you through Discord securely.
  • Display personalized dashboard stats, session history, and leaderboards.
  • Manage private rooms and study session settings.
  • Improve the service and provide stable bot operation.

Data Storage and Security

Your study records and account data are stored safely in our database. We do not sell your personal information. Access is limited to what is required to run BrainHive and support your experience.

Cookies and Sessions

BrainHive uses cookies to maintain login sessions and keep your dashboard connected to your account. Cookies are used only for authentication and session handling.

Third-Party Services

We rely on Discord OAuth for authentication and may use trusted hosting providers to deliver the website. We do not share your private information beyond these service requirements.

Your Rights

You may request data removal or correction by contacting the BrainHive administrators through the Discord community. We will respond to legitimate requests in a timely manner.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please visit our Discord server or contact support through the community channels linked on the site.
